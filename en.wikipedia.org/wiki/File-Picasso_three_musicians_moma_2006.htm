Summary[edit]

Photograph of the Painting "Musiciens aux masques" (Musicians with Masks) by Pablo Picasso in the Museum of Modern Art, New York City, New York, USA taken by Rolf Müller on February 11, 2006 using a Canon Inc. EOS 350D digital camera with an EFS 18-55mm lens.
