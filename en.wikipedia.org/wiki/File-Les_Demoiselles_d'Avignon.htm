Summary[edit]

Description

Les Demoiselles d'Avignon, oil on canvas, 244 x 234 cm

Source

Museum of Modern Art, New York

Date

Pablo Picasso completed Les Demoiselles d'Avignon in 1907. Les Demoiselles d'Avignon appeared in the May 1910 edition of the Architectural Record, a US publication [1]. Although it was labeled a "study", numerous sources confirm that the painting itself was reproduced therein (please see File talk:Les Demoiselles d'Avignon.jpg#2008 renewal of discussion). It wasn't published in France until 1925, when it appeared in La Révolution surréaliste [2].

Author

Pablo Picasso
